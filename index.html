Our Story

Two Bankers, One Shared Conviction

SPACE DYNAMICS is a boutique CRE advisory company specializing in very niche and select commercial real estate projects.

Both left Kotak in early 2003 — one joined The Times of India, the other joined Cushman & Wakefield. Exactly five years later, in 2008, the two reunited to start SPACE DYNAMICS. At the time, one was heading the Real Estate Exhibitions division at TOI, and the other was Retail West-Head at C&W.

The firm began as a retail-spaces leasing specialist — a rare focus at a time when most agencies were generalists handling apartments, land and industrial space alongside retail. The duo stuck to retail, and as the team grew, doubled down on their love for retail leasing. Soon, "Lease and Sale of Commercial Spaces" became the holy grail for SPACE DYNAMICS.
